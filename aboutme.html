Get to know me:

I’ve always loved words and the art of a good story. When I was nine, I started my own neighborhood newspaper called “The Buzzing Times,” painstakingly typing out “articles” on my mom’s antique typewriter at our scratched kitchen table, erasing typos with a goopy whiteout brush, impatiently blowing on it so it dried faster. I interviewed all of our neighbors about the (sometimes non-existent) happenings on our small-town street. (Spoiler alert: I’m from a small farm town in Connecticut, there’s not a lot going on besides a missing cat and a runaway cow!).


My brief foray into the world of newspapers lit something inside of me. I discovered how much I love talking to people and telling their stories. Whether it’s through words or design, my passion is breaking through the clutter and clearly communication messages succinctly…and with a little flair.
